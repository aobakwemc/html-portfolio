The Best Movies I've Enjoyed By AMC

My top 3 movies as of date.

Scarface

Honestly man, past the gruesomeness and violence of the movie, Tony Montana was such a wholesome funny character to watch.

Goodfellas

This movie just encompasses Italian Mafia and its lifestyle. What I liked most about it was the screen acting and the amount of quotables this came from that movie. Robert De Niro was a delight!

Casino

Once again... Rob, Rob, Robert De Niro. What an actor man! Funny guy! Cool, calm & collected. Worth the watch.
